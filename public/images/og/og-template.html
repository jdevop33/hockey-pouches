How to create your OpenGraph image:

  1. Replace the placeholder logo with your actual PNG logo by putting a file named "hockey-logo.png" in the /public/images/og/ directory.
  2. Take a screenshot of the image above (use a browser extension or your OS screenshot tool).
  3. Crop the screenshot to exactly 1200x630 pixels.
  4. Save it as "hockey-pouches-og.png" in the /public/images/og/ directory.
  5. Update your layout.tsx file to use this new image for OpenGraph and Twitter cards.
